Can I make a cash donation?

While not impossible, our team strongly encourages other transaction methods because cash donations require more administrative oversight. Please email donate@pfs.org.sg for details.

Tax deductible giving

Donations to PFS are not tax deductible. Donations to Seventy Times Seven are tax deductible.

Guidelines for Fundraising and Donor Solicitation

  • Integrity and Transparency: Fundraising activities must be conducted with honesty, transparency, and respect for donors and the community.
  • Compliance and Governance: Activities must comply with relevant laws and approved plans.
  • Accountability: PFS and 70x7 are accountable for the proper management of funds and items received.
  • Respect for Donors: Donors are treated with respect and confidentiality.
  • Financial Management and Reporting: Funds are responsibly managed with proper records and regular audits.
