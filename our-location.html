Getting Here

When you enter Parish of Christ Church premises, head to the Reverend Canon Samuel Baboo Block, Level 3.

By Train

Nearest MRT Stations are Little India & Novena.

You may consider taking a bus from Little India MRT station (NE 7 / DT 12): use Exit A, head to bus stop no. 40011, then take bus 56, 57, 166, 851 or 980 for two stops and alight at bus stop no. 50011.

From Novena MRT station (NS 20), use Exit B, head to bus stop no. 50038, then take bus 56, 57, 166, 851 or 980 for two stops and alight at bus stop no. 50019.

By Bus

Bus stop no. 50019 (Parish of Christ Ch): 56, 57, 166, 851, 980.

Bus stop no. 50011 (Opp Parish of Christ Ch): 56, 57, 166, 851, 980.

Bus stop no. 50351 (Farrer Pk Tennis Ctr): 131, 131A.

By Car

Weekday car parking is available for PFS guests only. Weekend car parking is not allowed.

Weekend visitors are kindly advised to park elsewhere nearby. Additional lots are available at Kandang Kerbau Hospital and near the Farrer Park Field.
